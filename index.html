Peter finds Mary Jane

The villains have captured Mary Jane, will Spider-man (you) be able to rescue her?

Instructions

Try to kill some of the most famous villains Spider-man has faced during his travel and you probably will find Mary Jane at some point.

Score

There will appear some kind of air or ground enemies along your way. Each killed enemy will increase your total score by 25 points. Kill as much of them as you can!

Gifts

Mary Jane will be happier if you collect some of the special gifts that will appear on the air. Each of them will increase your "Gifts" marker by one.
