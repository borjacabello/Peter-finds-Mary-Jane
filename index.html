Peter finds Mary Jane

The villains have captured Mary Jane, will Spider-man (you) be able to rescue her?

Instructions

Try to kill some of the most famous villains Spider-Man has faced during his travel and you probably will find Mary Jane at some moment (she will appear in less than a minute). Be cautious! You only have 3 lives.

There will appear some kind of air or ground enemies along your way. Each killed enemy will increase your total score by 10 points. Kill as much of them as you can!

Here below you can choose which Spiderman to play with, a background city for your adventure and the most suitable level for you. DEFAULT: red Spider-Man, night city and easy level.
